My Crafting Story

How I fell in love with paper and home decor

Hi, I'm Kidist!

I'm someone who truly loves creativity and turning simple materials into beautiful art. Paper craft and home décor are not just hobbies for me they are a way to express my imagination and bring life into my space.

Through my passion for home décor, I love designing spaces that feel warm, cozy, and inspiring. I believe even small handmade details can completely transform a room and make it feel like home.

I enjoy making handmade decorations like paper flowers, wall hangings, greeting cards, and DIY crafts.

There is something very special about creating something unique with my own hands. It makes every piece feel personal and meaningful.
