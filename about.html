My Crafting Story

How I fell in love with paper and home decor

Hi, I'm Kidist!

I started making paper decorations during lockdown – first simple snowflakes, then origami, then full wall art. Now my room is full of colourful paper flowers, garlands, and handmade frames. I love that you can create something beautiful from almost nothing.

What Inspires My Crafts

Nature (leaves, flowers), Japanese origami, and upcycling old books. I also love looking at interior design photos and thinking: "I can make that with paper!"
